Common questions

Who counts as a "school site"?

Each school, continuation school, alternative education site, or district office location with administrators who will use Trackument counts as one site. If you're unsure, contact us and we'll help you figure it out.

How many administrators can use it?

Unlimited. Every principal, assistant principal, and district administrator at your included sites can use Trackument at no additional cost.

Can we pay by purchase order?

Yes. Select "Purchase Order / Check" above and we'll send a formal invoice within 24 hours. Access is activated once payment is received.

Is our data private?

Employee names, administrator names, and dates are replaced with placeholders before any information reaches the AI. Real employee names never leave your browser. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What if we need to add sites mid-year?

Contact us and we'll send a prorated invoice for the additional sites. Your access updates immediately.
